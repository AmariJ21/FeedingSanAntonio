The Problem: Why It Matters

Every day, around 250,000 people in San Antonio go hungry, and many of them are children, seniors, and families who never expected to be in this situation. Hunger takes away more than a meal, because it slowly wears down a person's dignity, hope, and sense of security. When we choose to care and give, we help restore that hope and remind people that their lives truly matter.

Hunger doesn't discriminate. It affects our homeless neighbors sleeping under bridges, working families choosing between rent and groceries, and seniors on fixed incomes. In San Antonio, the gap between need and resources grows wider each day. Your donation directly feeds those who have nowhere else to turn, providing nutritious meals and restoring dignity to lives touched by hardship.

Emergency Food Assistance

When someone is facing hunger, they cannot wait days or weeks for help. Our Emergency Food Assistance program provides immediate meals so people in crisis can feel relief, comfort, and the reassurance that they are not alone. Your support helps us respond the moment hunger appears, turning fear into hope and compassion into action.

Family Nutrition Programs

Families shouldn't have to choose between paying bills and feeding their children, yet that's the impossible decision many face daily. Our Family Nutrition Programs provide consistent food support, nutritional education, and resources to help families build long-term food security. We serve everyone from single parents working multiple jobs to homeless families seeking stability, ensuring no one falls through the cracks.

School Meal Support

Hungry children cannot focus, learn, or dream about their future. Our School Meal Support program ensures that every child, including those from homeless families living in shelters or cars, receives nutritious breakfast and lunch during the school week. We also provide weekend food packs so children don't go hungry when school is out. Education begins with a full stomach.

Senior Nutrition Services

Our seniors deserve to live with dignity, not struggle to afford food after a lifetime of contribution. Many elderly residents face impossible choices between medication and meals, while homeless seniors are among our most vulnerable population. Our Senior Nutrition Services delivers hot meals directly to homebound seniors and provides food assistance at community centers, ensuring our elders never go hungry or feel forgotten.
